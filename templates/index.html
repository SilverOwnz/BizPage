    About Us

    Our Project Guide

    Our Mission

    To provide easy access to restaurants that are recommended by people around us.

    Our Plan

    We are creating a platform where people are able to interact with each other and relay information easily.

    Our Vision

    We would like to establish a more friendly app that everybody understands how to use and have a great time using it.

    Services

    We provide services for Restaurant Owners, Customers.

    Restaurant Owners

    You can post up your Restaurants on the Listings page and users will be able to go over and view your restaurant.

    Customers

    For normal users, we are able to find new chill-out spots around Singapore and recommend the place to others if you do like it. Please do give feedback about the place and rate it after visiting it.
